About the Patient Management Application

Patient Management is a platform designed for healthcare professionals. It allows for efficient patient tracking through the following features:

  • Patient Management: Track medical information, diagnoses, and treatment history.
  • Appointment Scheduling: Organize appointments and schedule medical visits.
  • Medical Follow-up: Monitor treatment progress and patients' health status.
  • Secure Authentication: Ensure secure access to sensitive information.
  • Reports and Statistics: Generate detailed medical reports for comprehensive follow-up.

This application aims to streamline the management of healthcare services by centralizing all necessary information and optimizing healthcare professionals' time.
